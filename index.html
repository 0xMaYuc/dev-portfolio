About Me

My name is Sebastian

A passionate builder, critical thinker, and dedicated globetrotter, I love creating things, designing concepts and executing on them – no matter whether in a digital or real-world set up.

My journey as a builder started in the digital space, where I helped businesses deliver an outstanding experience though cutting-edge web design in the early 2000s. Assuming different roles at various businesses such the German Automobile Association ADAC, software development specialist SunnySystems, and Voycer, a leading local provider of Direct2Customer digital solutions, I was able to constantly hone my skills in the field.

Having spent more than 10 years in web development, however, I decided to take a different route and set off to the Americas. Travelling the continent for several years I eventually settled to become a landlord in the countryside of the Peninsula of Yucatan (Mexico). After 10 years of work making the place comfortable for living and building cabañas to receive tourists, I felt it’s time for another take on my earlier self.

Starting 2021, I took a deep dive into revitalizing my skills in modern concepts of web development with a strong focus on responsive design, CSS3 animations, and more.

I’d be more than excited to get the chance to try and prove my skills on a professional level.
